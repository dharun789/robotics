ROBOTICS AND DRONES

S.No Project Title Discription
01 Autonomous Agriculture Assistants Develop robotics and drones to assist farmers in tasks such as crop monitoring, pesticide application, and yield estimation, enhancing agricultural efficiency
02 Search and Rescue Swarm Design a swarm of autonomous drones for search and rescue operations, capable of quickly and efficiently locating individuals in disaster stricken areas.
03 RoboHealth: Medical Assistance Robots Create robotic assistants for healthcare settings to aid in patient care, medication delivery, and hospital logistics, reducing the workload on healthcare professionals.
04 Drones for Wildlife Conservation Develop drones equipped with sensors and AI to monitor and protect wildlife, providing valuable data for conservation efforts and preventing illegal activities.
05 Robotic Inspection Crew Build robotic systems for infrastructure inspection in challenging environments, such as underwater pipelines, bridges, and high rise buildings, ensuring safety and efficiency.
06 Drone Based Environmental Monitoring Implement drone technology to monitor and collect data on environmental factors, such as air quality, water pollution, and deforestation, for better environmental management.
07 Robotics in Education Create educational robots to enhance STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) learning for students at various levels, promoting hands on exploration.
08 Drones for Precision Logistics Develop drones for precise and efficient delivery of goods, optimizing logistics operations in urban and remote areas, especially during emergencies.
09 Robotic Assistance for People with Disabilities Design robotic systems to assist individuals with disabilities in daily tasks, providing increased mobility, communication support, and improved independence.
10 Drone based Urban Planning Implement drones for urban planning and infrastructure development, collecting data for traffic analysis, land use mapping, and city expansion.
11 Aquatic Exploration Robotics Develop underwater robots for marine exploration, enabling scientists to study ocean ecosystems, map underwater terrain, and monitor marine life.
12 Drones for Emergency Response Create drones equipped with medical supplies and communication devices for rapid deployment in emergency situations, facilitating quick response and aid delivery.
13 Robotic Entertainment Companions Design interactive and entertaining robots for companionship, especially for individuals who may be isolated or in need of social interaction.
14 Drones for Precision Farming Develop drones equipped with advanced sensors and AI for precision farming practices, optimizing crop health monitoring, and resource management.
15 Robotics in Construction Create robotic solutions for construction tasks, such as bricklaying, concrete pouring, and site inspection, to improve efficiency and safety in the construction industry.
16 Student Innovation Student can submit their own ideas of their interest.
